Welcome to Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery



Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a sanctuary for sea turtles that need to be protected and conserved. Visitors can learn about the life cycle of sea turtles and participate in releasing baby turtles into the ocean. Additionally, the hatchery has a museum that showcases sea turtle conservation and environmental education exhibits.




Things to do at Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

  • Learn about sea turtle conservation

  • See and touch sea turtles

  • Participate in releasing baby turtles into the ocean

  • Visit the museum
