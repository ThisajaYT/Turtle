Bentota Turtle Hatchery

Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a dedicated sanctuary and conservation center for sea turtles located in the picturesque coastal town of Bentota, Sri Lanka. Our hatchery is driven by a profound commitment to protect and preserve these magnificent marine creatures and their habitats. We play a vital role in the conservation of sea turtles through various efforts, including rescuing vulnerable eggs, providing a safe environment for the hatchlings to develop, and releasing them back into the ocean. Our mission is not only to ensure the survival of sea turtles but also to raise awareness about their conservation among visitors, local communities, and schools.

About Bentota Turtle Hatchery

Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a sanctuary and conservation center dedicated to the preservation of sea turtles. Located in the beautiful coastal town of Bentota, Sri Lanka, our hatchery focuses on protecting and safeguarding sea turtles and their habitats. We rescue vulnerable sea turtle eggs from nesting beaches, providing them with a safe environment to hatch and develop. Our experienced staff and dedicated volunteers closely monitor the eggs, ensuring optimal conditions for their successful incubation. Once the hatchlings emerge, we nurture them until they are strong enough to be released into the ocean, where they can contribute to the survival of their species. Additionally, we actively promote awareness and education about the importance of sea turtle conservation, working with visitors, local communities, and schools to foster a sense of environmental stewardship. At Bentota Turtle Hatchery, we are committed to preserving these ancient creatures and ensuring their long-term survival in our oceans.

Volunteering and Supporting us

If you are interested in supporting or volunteering at the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, here are some ways you can get involved:

1. Make a donation: The hatchery relies on donations to support its conservation efforts. You can make a one-time or recurring donation on their website or in person at the hatchery.

2. Adopt a turtle: The hatchery offers an adoption program that allows you to symbolically adopt a sea turtle. You will receive a certificate and updates on your turtle's progress.

3. Volunteer at the hatchery: The hatchery welcomes volunteers to help with a variety of tasks, including cleaning tanks, feeding turtles, and assisting with beach patrols. Contact the hatchery to inquire about volunteer opportunities and availability.

4. Spread the word: Help raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation by sharing information about the hatchery on social media, in your community, or with friends and family.

5. Visit the hatchery: By visiting the hatchery and taking a guided tour, you will not only learn more about the conservation efforts, but also support the hatchery through your admission fee.

Your support and contribution can help the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery continue its important work to protect sea turtles and their habitats.
