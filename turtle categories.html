Turtle Categories



Olive Ridley Turtle

The Olive Ridley turtle is one of the smallest sea turtles, measuring around 2 feet in length and weighing up to 100 pounds. They are named after their olive-colored shells and can be found in the waters around Sri Lanka.

Green Turtle

The Green turtle is one of the largest sea turtles, measuring up to 4 feet in length and weighing up to 500 pounds. They are named after their green-colored fat and can be found in the waters around Sri Lanka.

Hawksbill Turtle

The Hawksbill turtle is a medium-sized sea turtle, measuring around 2-3 feet in length and weighing up to 150 pounds. They are named after their pointed beaks and can be found in the waters around Sri Lanka.
